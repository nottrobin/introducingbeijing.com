Day Two

Visit the Forbidden City, the JingShan Park, the Houhai and the Beijing Hutongs

We could start with the Forbidden City. The earlier the better as it can get quite crowded. It will probably be near to where you're staying so 9am would be a good time to start.

The Forbidden City has several museums inside, as well as being quite extensive. Two and a half hours would probably be sufficient to see the place.

Then we can climb the hill behind the city - JingShan Park - from which you get good views over the city.

We could then lunch at Houhai, which has restaurants beside a beautiful lake.

In the afternoon, you can take a rickshaw ride through the Hutongs if you like.

In the evening you may wish to go to a famous duck restaurant which we can organize for you.

So, if you think we could help you please get in touch.
