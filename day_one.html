Day One

Visit the Summer Palace and the Botanical Gardens

We would leave hotel at 9.30am. The journey takes about an hour by car from central Beijing.

The Summer Palace is the largest garden in Asia, arranged around a lake, with numerous palaces and terraces. Includes a Marble Boat and boat trips if you wish.

Lunch near the Summer Palace - We can find you a good restaurant.

The Botanical gardens are on the way back, about 10km from Summer Palace.

They are not large, so won't take very long, but they have many beautiful and rare plants.

In the evening, you may wish to go to see the Beijing Opera (singing and costume), or acrobatics, or 'Chun Yi: The Legend of Kung Fu'. All are good spectacles.
